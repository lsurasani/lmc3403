What is Technology Consulting?

Technology consulting is a field that advises organizations on how to use technology to achieve their objectives.

What credentials do you need to become a tech consultant?

Typically, tech consultants have Bachelor's degrees in computer science, some concentration or experience in IT, or an engineering field.
